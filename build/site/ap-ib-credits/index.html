AP and IB CS credits

Students who have taken Computer Science courses can obtain credit and waive courses within the Computer Science curriculum.  The current set of equivalencies across the State of Oregon can be found for the Advanced Placement (AP) Computer Science exams here and the International Baccalaureate Computer Science courses here .

Currently, we allow the following:

  • AP Computer Science Principles (3 or above): CS 160
  • AP Computer Science A (3 or above): CS 161
  • IB Standard Computer Science (4 or above): CS 160
  • IB Higher Level Computer Science (4 or above): CS 161
