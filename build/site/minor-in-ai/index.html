Minor in Artificial Intelligence

Program Requirements

The AI minor coursework enables students to learn how Artificial Intelligence systems work and their application towards solving societal problems. Earning an AI minor gives students seeking careers in liberal arts, business, health, and other fields an opportunity to increase their competitiveness in our increasingly technology-driven workplace.  Note that the courses within the Minor will eventually be integrated within the Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence.

To earn a minor in Artificial Intelligence, a student must complete the course of study below (24 credits):

  • AI 112: Generative AI Studio (4)
  • AI 141: Foundations: Computing Systems (4)
  • AI 142: Foundations: Computation (4)
  • CS 161: Introduction to Programming and Problem-Solving (4)
  • AI 241: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (4)
  • Approved lower division AI elective (4)

For guidance on how to add the minor to your degree program or graduation application, or to ask questions relating to course selection, please contact our Undergraduate Advisor, at uccs@pdx.edu .
