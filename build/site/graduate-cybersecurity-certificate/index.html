Graduate Cybersecurity Certificate

The Graduate Cybersecurity Certificate program requires admission as a graduate student. The program requires 21 hours total of graduate classes. There are two core classes for a total of 6 hours. In addition, five elective classes must be taken for the needed additional 15 credit hours.

For more about these requirements, students may go to the most recent catalog (also known as the PSU Bulletin), which includes a section specifically for the Graduate Cybersecurity Certificate .

For detailed information about the Cybersecurity Graduate Certificate, please consult the Graduate Handbook .
